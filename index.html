Farmer's Plastic Pollution App

This app is designed to help farmers reduce their plastic footprint.

Here are some of the features of the app:

  • Find and connect with other farmers who are interested in reusing, recycling, and reducing plastic materials.
  • Find new ways to use old plastic materials, such as turning them into compost or building materials.
  • Get information on recycling programs and other resources that can help you reduce your plastic waste.

To get started, please create an account or log in if you already have an account.
